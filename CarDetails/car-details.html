Summary about Mercedes-Amg-GT53

The 2025 Mercedes-AMG GT sedan offers a sleek, high-performance alternative to the GT Coupe, with added practicality thanks to its four-door design. Though based on the E-Class rather than the SL roadster, it shares a similar aggressive styling. Available in GT43, GT53, and GT63 trims, it features a turbocharged inline-six in the lower models, while the GT63 and GT63 S boast a twin-turbo V8, delivering up to 630 hp. A plug-in hybrid variant pushes power to 831 hp. Despite limited rear headroom, the GT sedan blends luxury and performance in an exclusive market segment.
